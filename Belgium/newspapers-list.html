All Belgium Newspapers

Belgium is a country situated in Western Europe. Belgium is one of the founding members of the European Union. The country is of mainly two groups of people the Dutch and the French. There are also a few German speaking groups in Belgium. Dutch, French and German are recognized as the official languages of Belgium. There are a lot of daily newspapers in the country. Most of the newspapers are based on the regional languages either French or Dutch. There are also a lot of local newspapers in Belgium. There are small number of newspapers in English and German too. Fashion and sports magazines are present in Belgium in high numbers.
